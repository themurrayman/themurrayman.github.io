Destinations

So much to see

Taking you there

IBAT Tours offers a wide range of must-see destinations across three continents. No one takes you to more places at such affordable prices.

Europe

Enjoy a city break at Europe's top locations. Lowest prices for travel and accommodation.

Dublin

Ireland's capital is home to such attraction as Trinity College, the Phoenix Park, St Patrick's Cathedral, Dublin Castle and Temple Bar.

Berlin

Germany's capital and largest city is known for its historic buildings, contemporary arts scene, and modern landmarks.

Florence

At the centre of Italy's Tuscany region, Florence is home to many celebrated masterpieces of Renaissance art and architecture.

Lisbon

Portugal's hilly, coastal capital city. Enjoy the pastel-coloured buildings, cobbled streets and the custard tarts.

America

Long-haul holidays at prices you won't believe. See what America has to offer.

New York

Its iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building, the sprawling Central Park, and neon-lit Times Square.

Washington

Located on the Potomac River and contains such impressive buildings as the Capitol, White House and Supreme Court.

New Orleans

Nicknamed the "Big Easy," this vibrant city is known for its round-the-clock nightlife, live music scene, and spicy cuisine.

Seattle

Surrounded by water, mountains and evergreen forests, Seattle is home to a large tech industry and contains thousands of acres of parkland.

Australia

Take a trip to the Land Down Under. Australia's premier destinations are waiting for you.

Sydney

A vibrant city that shines all year round with waterside bars and cafes, spectacular events, music and ideas.

Melbourne

The city's events calendar is packed with music, film and design festivals, sports events, and food and wine celebrations.

Ayer's Rock

Traditionally known as Uluru, Ayer's Rock is a world-famous sandstone monolith in the heart of Australia.

Perth

Sandy beaches line Perth's suburbs, and the huge, riverside Kings Park and Botanic Garden on Mount Eliza offer sweeping views.
